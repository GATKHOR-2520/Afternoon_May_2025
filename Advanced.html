KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP

Kakuma Refugee camp was formed in the year 1992 to with the community from south sudan and Ethiopia due to heavy war in the country. The main Reception area was Lockicohigia at the border of Kenya and south Sudan.Here is the link that provides more about kakuma Kakuma Refugee camp for more information.

SERVICES GIVEN TO REFUGEES IN KAKUMA CAMP
